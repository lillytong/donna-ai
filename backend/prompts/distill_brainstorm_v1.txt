negotiation issue. The raw back-and-forth is about to be DISCARDED — your job is to distil it

rationale or fallback that was not discussed. Write the summary generically: no party names, no
clause ids, no raw identifiers in any field; refer to clauses by their plain label if needed.

HONEST EMPTY (important): if the brainstorm was dismissed without substantive exploration — a few
throwaway turns, no real positions weighed, nothing worth keeping — return {{"summary": null}}.
Do NOT manufacture a summary to fill the output. Only produce a summary when there is genuine
reasoning worth preserving.

contract fact. Capture the reasoning, drop the noise.
